Bullhorn 60-day gate: only gate out a company if there is ACTIVE ENGAGEMENT in the last 60 days.

Active engagement means ONLY:
- New job/vacancy added
- New placement added
- Meetings logged
- Connected calls tracked

NOT active engagement (do NOT gate on these):
- LinkedIn messages or BD messages sent
- Emails sent
- Attempted calls (not connected)
- Mailshots / bulk outreach
- Company merely existing as "Prospect"

**Why:** Outreach alone does not grant client ownership at Signify. Someone sending LinkedIn messages doesn't mean they own the account. Only real engagement (jobs, placements, meetings, connected calls) proves active work.

**How to apply:** When checking Bullhorn gate, look for new jobs, placements, meetings, or connected calls in last 60 days. Ignore all other note types and outreach activity.
